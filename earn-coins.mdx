This guide will show you how to earn coins via Linkvertise.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1.">
    <Card title="Go to the dashboard." icon="link" href="https://dash.wraithnodes.net">
      Clicking on this button will redirect you to the dashboard.
    </Card>
    <img
        className="block rounded-md"
        src="/images/earncoins/1.jpg"
        alt="Step 1"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 2.">
    On the sidebar on the left, click on the "Earn Coins" button, or use the button below to automatically redirect to the Earn Coins page.

    <Card title="Go to the Earn Coins section" icon="link" href="https://dash.wraithnodes.net/earn">
      Clicking on this button will redirect you to the Earn Coins page on the dashboard.
    </Card>

    <img
        className="block rounded-md"
        src="/images/earncoins/2.jpg"
        alt="Step 2"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 3.">
    Click on the "Get Website" button.

    <img
        className="block rounded-md"
        src="/images/earncoins/3.jpg"
        alt="Step 3"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 4.">
    You should now see some ads that may appear once you click on the "Get Website" button. Skip them by clicking on the top right of the ad that says "Skip".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 5.">
    You should now see a countdown that starts from 10 seconds. Wait until this countdown finishes.

    <Warning>Some users report the countdown being over an hour long. We unfortunately cannot do anything about that, since it is a Linkvertise issue. If you receive a countdown that is over an hour long, try again later.</Warning>

    <img
        className="block rounded-md"
        src="/images/earncoins/4.jpg"
        alt="Step 5"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 6.">
    Once the countdown finishes, click on the "Get Website" button.

    <img
        className="block rounded-md"
        src="/images/earncoins/5.jpg"
        alt="Step 6"
    />
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Check>Congrats! You have successfully earned free coins from Linkvertise. Friendly reminder that you need to renew your server every 24 hours with 15 coins, so this method is the easiest way to get coins.</Check>
